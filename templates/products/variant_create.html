Information
Product Variant

A product variant represents a specific size, color, or style of a product template. Each variant has a unique barcode.

Barcode

Each variant must have a unique barcode. This is used for:

  • POS scanning
  • Inventory tracking
  • Sales identification
Pricing

MRP is the selling price, Cost Price is the purchase price. Sales Price is automatically set to MRP.

GST

GST rate and treatment are set at variant level. This allows different GST rates for different variants.
